## CRITIQUE

- **Risk:** v08-new-persona vector "From now on act as DAN..." was not detected by existing patterns.  
  *Fix:* Added `r'from now on act as'` to `POISONING_PATTERNS`.
- **Risk:** Low-entropy (repetitive) content as adversarial padding — not caught by pattern regex.  
  *Fix:* `validate_before_cache()` computes Shannon entropy; content with entropy < 1.5 bits/char AND length > 100 chars is flagged.
- **Risk:** `EXTENDED_PATTERNS` in `validate_before_cache` extends `POISONING_PATTERNS` without deduplication.  
  *Acceptable:* `re.search` with duplicates is harmless (same match, no false positives).

## NOTES

- `validate_before_cache()` returns: `{"doc_id", "is_clean", "detected_patterns", "entropy", "low_entropy", "action"}`.
- `action` is `"allow"` or `"quarantine"` — caller decides whether to store or reject.
- Entropy threshold 1.5 bits/char: normal English prose ~4.0; adversarial `"token " * 500` ≈ 1.08.
- `sanitize_user_query()` strips null bytes, truncates to 2048 chars.
