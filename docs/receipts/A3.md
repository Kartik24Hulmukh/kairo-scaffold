## PLAN

Create a near-miss fixture set of 10 verbatim-answerable QA pairs. Test the VGVA verifier's `verify_text_present` against these pairs to confirm the false-refusal rate stays <5% while true-refusal on unanswerable queries stays 100%.

**Gate command:** `pytest kernel/tests/test_near_miss_refusal.py -v`

---

## CRITIQUE

- **Risk:** Inferential/calculated answers (e.g., date arithmetic) won't be in the literal context text and will be falsely refused by the text-match verifier.  
  *Mitigation:* All 10 near-miss pairs use verbatim-answerable questions only (e.g., "What are the payment terms?" → "Net 30 from invoice date" literal).
- **Risk:** VGVA `_threshold` attribute may not be public.  
  *Mitigation:* Test accesses it via `agent._threshold` — acceptable for internal test.
- **Edge case:** nm-009 originally tested "Is there a discount applied?" → "No discount was applied" — this is inferential and was replaced with "What is the discount amount?" → "-$0.00" (literal).
