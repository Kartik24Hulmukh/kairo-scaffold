SUBJECT: Assessment of Weapons Procurement Networks in Eastern Mediterranean Region

REFERENCES:
(a) CAG Report 2003-0142, "Maritime Interdiction Results Q1 2003"
(b) DNI Briefing Note 2003-0088, "Proliferation Trends in the Levant"
(c) SIGINT Summary 2003-0201 (TS//SI//NOFORN)

1. (S//NF) This memorandum summarizes findings from the joint analysis of procurement
networks operating in the Eastern Mediterranean, with particular focus on port
activities in Latakia and Tartus, Syria.

2. (S) Key entities identified in this assessment:
   - PERSON: Viktor Antonovich Petrov, suspected intermediary based in Limassol, Cyprus
   - ORG: Al-Rashid Trading Company, Damascus, Syria
   - ORG: Meridian Shipping LLC, registered in Nicosia, Cyprus
   - LOCATION: Port of Latakia, Syria
   - LOCATION: Free Trade Zone, Tartus, Syria
   - EQUIPMENT: Type-63 107mm Multiple Rocket Launchers (qty 24, estimated)

3. (S//NF) Analysis of shipping manifests obtained through HUMINT source CARAVEL-7
indicates a pattern of transshipments through Limassol to Latakia between January
and March 2003. End-user certificates appear fraudulent.

4. (C) Recommendations:
   a. Increase maritime surveillance in the Eastern Mediterranean corridor
   b. Coordinate with allied SIGINT partners for communications monitoring
   c. Brief the National Security Council on proliferation risk assessment
