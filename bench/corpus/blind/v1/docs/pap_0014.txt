ViT: An Image is Worth 16x16 Words

Alexey Dosovitskiy

Abstract
We present a novel approach to vit. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Contributions:
- Our method achieves state-of-the-art results on ImageNet.
- We introduce a new architecture that reduces training time by 57%.
- Our approach generalizes to unseen domains without additional training.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 33 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 3.36 and sFID 2.71. The precision score is 0.10.
