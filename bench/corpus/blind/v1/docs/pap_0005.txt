Abstract
We present a novel approach to gpt-4 technical report. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Main Results:
1. Our model achieves FID 2.83 on the primary benchmark.
2. Performance improves with scale following a power law.
3. The proposed method outperforms all baselines by a significant margin.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 34 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 1.84 and sFID 3.15. The precision score is 0.43.
