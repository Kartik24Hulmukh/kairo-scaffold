LaMDA: Language Models for Dialog Applications

Romal Thoppil, Daniel De Freitas

Abstract
We present a novel approach to lamda. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Main Results:
1. Our model achieves FID 3.05 on the primary benchmark.
2. Performance improves with scale following a power law.
3. The proposed method outperforms all baselines by a significant margin.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 9 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 1.42 and sFID 2.97. The precision score is 0.92.
