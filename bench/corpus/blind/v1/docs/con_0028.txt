NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement is entered into between Apex Logistics Co and Zenith Transport, effective 2024-02-24.

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Apex Logistics Co shall deliver professional services as described in Schedule A. Zenith Transport shall remit payment within 45 days of receipt of each invoice.

2. NON-DISCLOSURE
Both parties covenant and agree that all proprietary or confidential information shared during the engagement shall be held in strict confidence.

3. TERM
This Agreement commences on 2024-02-24 and shall remain in effect until 2034-12-06, unless terminated earlier.

4. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Delaware.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 60 days written notice.
