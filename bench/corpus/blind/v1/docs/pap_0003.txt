Attention Is All You Need

Ashish Vaswani, Noam Shazeer, Niki Parmar

Abstract
We present a novel approach to attention is all you need. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Claims:
1. Our model achieves 0.91 accuracy on the test set.
2. We show that the proposed method is more efficient than prior work.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 10 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 3.10 and sFID 3.59. The precision score is 0.16.
