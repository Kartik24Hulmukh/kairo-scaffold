NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

Effective Date: 2024-05-28

The parties to this agreement are:
1. Coral Reef Resorts, a limited partnership registered in the Cayman Islands
2. Pearl Hospitality, a company incorporated in Singapore

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Coral Reef Resorts shall deliver professional services as described in Schedule A. Pearl Hospitality shall remit payment within 30 days of receipt of each invoice.

2. NON-DISCLOSURE
Both parties covenant and agree that all proprietary or confidential information shared during the engagement shall be held in strict confidence.

3. TERM
This Agreement commences on 2024-05-28 and shall remain in effect until 2034-02-26, unless terminated earlier.

4. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Singapore.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 90 days written notice.
