CONSULTING AGREEMENT

This Consulting Agreement is entered into between Velvet Fashion Group and Silk Apparel Co, effective 2024-08-04.

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Velvet Fashion Group shall deliver professional services as described in Schedule A. Silk Apparel Co shall remit payment within 45 days of receipt of each invoice.

2. NON-DISCLOSURE
Both parties covenant and agree that all proprietary or confidential information shared during the engagement shall be held in strict confidence.

3. TERM
This Agreement commences on 2024-08-04 and shall remain in effect until 2029-06-21, unless terminated earlier.

4. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Ireland.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice.
