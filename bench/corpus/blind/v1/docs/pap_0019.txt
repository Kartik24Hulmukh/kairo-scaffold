PaLM: Scaling Language Modeling with Pathways

Aakanksha Chowdhery, Sharan Narang

Abstract
We present a novel approach to palm. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Claims:
1. Our model achieves 0.85 accuracy on the test set.
2. We show that the proposed method is more efficient than prior work.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 46 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 3.24 and sFID 1.58. The precision score is 0.17.
