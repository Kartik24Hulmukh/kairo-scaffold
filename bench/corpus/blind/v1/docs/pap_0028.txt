SAM: Segment Anything

Alexander Kirillov, Eric Mintun

Abstract
We present a novel approach to sam. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Contributions:
- Our method achieves state-of-the-art results on GLUE.
- We introduce a new architecture that reduces training time by 41%.
- Our approach generalizes to unseen domains without additional training.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 45 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 3.62 and sFID 3.42. The precision score is 0.84.
