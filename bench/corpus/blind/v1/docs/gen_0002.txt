Market Analysis: Electric Vehicle Adoption 2024

Overview
The electric vehicle (EV) market has experienced significant growth in 2024, with global sales reaching 14.2 million units. This represents a 35% year-over-year increase from 2023 figures.

Primary Conclusions:
- China remains the largest EV market with 8.1 million units sold
- European adoption accelerated due to new EU emissions regulations
- North American growth was slower at 18% YoY
- Battery costs declined to $98/kWh, approaching price parity with ICE vehicles

Strategic Implications:
The data suggests that traditional automakers must accelerate EV transition plans. Companies with established battery supply chains have a 2-3 year competitive advantage.
