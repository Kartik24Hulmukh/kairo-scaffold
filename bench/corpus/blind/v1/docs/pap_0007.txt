Chain-of-Thought Prompting Elicits Reasoning

Jason Wei, Xuezhi Wang

Abstract
We present a novel approach to chain-of-thought prompting elicits reasoning. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Claims:
1. Our model achieves 0.87 accuracy on the test set.
2. We show that the proposed method is more efficient than prior work.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 10 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 1.20 and sFID 2.34. The precision score is 0.69.
