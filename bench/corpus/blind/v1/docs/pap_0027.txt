Whisper: Robust Speech Recognition via Large-Scale Weak Supervision

Alec Radford

Abstract
We present a novel approach to whisper. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Claims:
1. Our model achieves 0.92 accuracy on the test set.
2. We show that the proposed method is more efficient than prior work.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 35 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 0.57 and sFID 1.15. The precision score is 0.86.
