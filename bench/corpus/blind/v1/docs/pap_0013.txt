CLIP: Learning Transferable Visual Models

Alec Radford, Jong Wook Kim

Abstract
We present a novel approach to clip. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Claims:
1. Our model achieves 0.99 accuracy on the test set.
2. We show that the proposed method is more efficient than prior work.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 23 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 3.09 and sFID 3.93. The precision score is 0.18.
