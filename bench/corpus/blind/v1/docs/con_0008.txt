EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement is dated as of the 20th day of March, 2024, by and between:
Apex Logistics Co, a limited liability company organized under the laws of the State of Singapore (hereinafter "Provider"),
and
Zenith Transport, a corporation incorporated in Singapore (hereinafter "Client").

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Apex Logistics Co shall deliver professional services as described in Schedule A. Zenith Transport shall remit payment within 30 days of receipt of each invoice.

2. NON-DISCLOSURE
Both parties covenant and agree that all proprietary or confidential information shared during the engagement shall be held in strict confidence.

3. TERM
This Agreement commences on 2024-03-20 and shall remain in effect until 2034-01-13, unless terminated earlier.

4. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Singapore.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 90 days written notice.
