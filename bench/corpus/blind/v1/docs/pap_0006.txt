Constitutional AI: Harmlessness from AI Feedback

Yuntao Bai, Saurav Kadavath

Abstract
We present a novel approach to constitutional ai. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Claims:
1. Our model achieves 0.94 accuracy on the test set.
2. We show that the proposed method is more efficient than prior work.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 12 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 4.76 and sFID 1.44. The precision score is 0.22.
