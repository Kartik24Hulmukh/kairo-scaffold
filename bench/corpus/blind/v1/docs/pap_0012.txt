DALL-E 3: Improving Image Generation

James Betker, Gabriel Goh

Abstract
We present a novel approach to dall-e 3. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Claims:
1. Our model achieves 0.97 accuracy on the test set.
2. We show that the proposed method is more efficient than prior work.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 26 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 3.22 and sFID 0.62. The precision score is 0.23.
