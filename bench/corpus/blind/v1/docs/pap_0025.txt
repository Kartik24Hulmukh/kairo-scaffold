Gemma: Open Models Based on Gemini Research

Gemma Team

Abstract
We present a novel approach to gemma. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Main Results:
1. Our model achieves FID 2.34 on the primary benchmark.
2. Performance improves with scale following a power law.
3. The proposed method outperforms all baselines by a significant margin.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 30 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 1.55 and sFID 3.99. The precision score is 0.05.
