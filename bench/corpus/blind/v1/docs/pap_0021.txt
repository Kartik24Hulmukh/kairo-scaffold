LLaMA: Open and Efficient Foundation Language Models

Hugo Touvron, Thibaut Lavril

Abstract
We present a novel approach to llama. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Contributions:
- Our method achieves state-of-the-art results on GLUE.
- We introduce a new architecture that reduces training time by 52%.
- Our approach generalizes to unseen domains without additional training.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 23 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 2.37 and sFID 2.67. The precision score is 0.47.
