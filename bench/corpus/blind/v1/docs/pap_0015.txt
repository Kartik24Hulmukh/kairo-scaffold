AlphaFold: Highly Accurate Protein Structure Prediction

John Jumper, Richard Evans

Abstract
We present a novel approach to alphafold. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Contributions:
- Our method achieves state-of-the-art results on GLUE.
- We introduce a new architecture that reduces training time by 62%.
- Our approach generalizes to unseen domains without additional training.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 12 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 4.36 and sFID 1.98. The precision score is 0.69.
