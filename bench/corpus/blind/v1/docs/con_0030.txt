CONSULTING AGREEMENT

Effective Date: 2024-06-06

The parties to this agreement are:
1. Cobalt Mining Ltd, a limited partnership registered in the Cayman Islands
2. Azure Minerals Inc, a company incorporated in England

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Cobalt Mining Ltd shall deliver professional services as described in Schedule A. Azure Minerals Inc shall remit payment within 45 days of receipt of each invoice.

2. NON-DISCLOSURE
Both parties covenant and agree that all proprietary or confidential information shared during the engagement shall be held in strict confidence.

3. TERM
This Agreement commences on 2024-06-06 and shall remain in effect until 2027-01-23, unless terminated earlier.

4. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of England.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice.
