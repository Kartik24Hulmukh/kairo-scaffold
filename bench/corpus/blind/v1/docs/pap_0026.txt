CodeLlama: Open Foundation Models for Code

Baptiste Roziere

Abstract
We present a novel approach to codellama. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Contributions:
- Our method achieves state-of-the-art results on ImageNet.
- We introduce a new architecture that reduces training time by 51%.
- Our approach generalizes to unseen domains without additional training.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 8 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 1.07 and sFID 4.84. The precision score is 0.34.
