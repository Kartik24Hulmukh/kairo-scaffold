EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

Effective Date: 2024-10-24

The parties to this agreement are:
1. Amber Foods Inc, a limited partnership registered in the Cayman Islands
2. Honey Restaurant Group, a company incorporated in New York

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Amber Foods Inc shall deliver professional services as described in Schedule A. Honey Restaurant Group shall remit payment within 30 days of receipt of each invoice.

2. NON-DISCLOSURE
Both parties covenant and agree that all proprietary or confidential information shared during the engagement shall be held in strict confidence.

3. TERM
This Agreement commences on 2024-10-24 and shall remain in effect until 2025-03-10, unless terminated earlier.

4. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of New York.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 90 days written notice.
