EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

Effective Date: 2024-10-12

The parties to this agreement are:
1. Nexus Software Group, a limited partnership registered in the Cayman Islands
2. Vertex Capital, a company incorporated in Illinois

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Nexus Software Group shall deliver professional services as described in Schedule A. Vertex Capital shall remit payment within 90 days of receipt of each invoice.

2. NON-DISCLOSURE
Both parties covenant and agree that all proprietary or confidential information shared during the engagement shall be held in strict confidence.

3. TERM
This Agreement commences on 2024-10-12 and shall remain in effect until 2029-09-17, unless terminated earlier.

4. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Illinois.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 60 days written notice.
