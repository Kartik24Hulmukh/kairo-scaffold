CONSULTING AGREEMENT

This Consulting Agreement is entered into between Nexus Software Group and Vertex Capital, effective 2024-01-12.

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Nexus Software Group shall deliver professional services as described in Schedule A. Vertex Capital shall remit payment within 45 days of receipt of each invoice.

2. NON-DISCLOSURE
Both parties covenant and agree that all proprietary or confidential information shared during the engagement shall be held in strict confidence.

3. TERM
This Agreement commences on 2024-01-12 and shall remain in effect until 2027-07-28, unless terminated earlier.

4. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Washington.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice.
