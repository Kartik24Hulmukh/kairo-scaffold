Phi-2: The Surprising Power of Small Models

Li Yuan, Qibin Chen

Abstract
We present a novel approach to phi-2. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Contributions:
- Our method achieves state-of-the-art results on SuperGLUE.
- We introduce a new architecture that reduces training time by 42%.
- Our approach generalizes to unseen domains without additional training.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 27 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 2.98 and sFID 2.95. The precision score is 0.46.
