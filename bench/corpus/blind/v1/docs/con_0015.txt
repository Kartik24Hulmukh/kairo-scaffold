PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

This Partnership Agreement is dated as of the 13th day of November, 2024, by and between:
Slate Education, a limited liability company organized under the laws of the State of New York (hereinafter "Provider"),
and
Chalkboard Learning, a corporation incorporated in New York (hereinafter "Client").

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Slate Education shall deliver professional services as described in Schedule A. Chalkboard Learning shall remit payment within 45 days of receipt of each invoice.

2. NON-DISCLOSURE
Both parties covenant and agree that all proprietary or confidential information shared during the engagement shall be held in strict confidence.

3. TERM
This Agreement commences on 2024-11-13 and shall remain in effect until 2029-12-07, unless terminated earlier.

4. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of New York.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 90 days written notice.
