Conformer: Convolution-augmented Transformer

Anmol Gulati, Jasmine Qin

Abstract
We present a novel approach to conformer. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Claims:
1. Our model achieves 0.86 accuracy on the test set.
2. We show that the proposed method is more efficient than prior work.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 25 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 3.18 and sFID 4.21. The precision score is 0.91.
