NeRF: Representing Scenes as Neural Radiance Fields

Ben Mildenhall, Pratul Srinivasan

Abstract
We present a novel approach to nerf. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Contributions:
- Our method achieves state-of-the-art results on SuperGLUE.
- We introduce a new architecture that reduces training time by 75%.
- Our approach generalizes to unseen domains without additional training.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 40 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 0.66 and sFID 0.81. The precision score is 0.63.
