MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Master Service Agreement is entered into between Omega Systems LLC and Delta Corporation, effective 2024-03-24.

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Omega Systems LLC shall deliver professional services as described in Schedule A. Delta Corporation shall remit payment within 60 days of receipt of each invoice.

2. NON-DISCLOSURE
Both parties covenant and agree that all proprietary or confidential information shared during the engagement shall be held in strict confidence.

3. TERM
This Agreement commences on 2024-03-24 and shall remain in effect until 2029-08-09, unless terminated earlier.

4. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Delaware.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 90 days written notice.
