Mistral 7B: The Best 7B Model to Date

Albert Q Jiang

Abstract
We present a novel approach to mistral 7b. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Contributions:
- Our method achieves state-of-the-art results on GLUE.
- We introduce a new architecture that reduces training time by 72%.
- Our approach generalizes to unseen domains without additional training.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 6 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 1.77 and sFID 1.84. The precision score is 0.59.
