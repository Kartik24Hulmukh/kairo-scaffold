Mixtral of Experts

Albert Q Jiang, Alexandre Sablayrolles

Abstract
We present a novel approach to mixtral of experts. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Claims:
1. Our model achieves 0.90 accuracy on the test set.
2. We show that the proposed method is more efficient than prior work.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 44 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 2.79 and sFID 4.46. The precision score is 0.86.
