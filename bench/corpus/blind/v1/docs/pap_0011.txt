Wav2Vec 2.0: A Framework for Self-Supervised Learning

Alexei Baevski, Yuhao Zhou

Abstract
We present a novel approach to wav2vec 2.0. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Contributions:
- Our method achieves state-of-the-art results on GLUE.
- We introduce a new architecture that reduces training time by 64%.
- Our approach generalizes to unseen domains without additional training.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 31 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 4.74 and sFID 2.75. The precision score is 0.49.
