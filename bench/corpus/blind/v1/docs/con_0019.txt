MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Master Service Agreement is entered into between Bronze Athletics and Copper Sports Equipment, effective 2024-12-17.

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Bronze Athletics shall deliver professional services as described in Schedule A. Copper Sports Equipment shall remit payment within 90 days of receipt of each invoice.

2. NON-DISCLOSURE
Both parties covenant and agree that all proprietary or confidential information shared during the engagement shall be held in strict confidence.

3. TERM
This Agreement commences on 2024-12-17 and shall remain in effect until 2027-10-26, unless terminated earlier.

4. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of England.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 60 days written notice.
