EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

Effective Date: 2024-12-19

The parties to this agreement are:
1. Silver Line Transport, a limited partnership registered in the Cayman Islands
2. Golden Rail Systems, a company incorporated in Delaware

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Silver Line Transport shall deliver professional services as described in Schedule A. Golden Rail Systems shall remit payment within 30 days of receipt of each invoice.

2. NON-DISCLOSURE
Both parties covenant and agree that all proprietary or confidential information shared during the engagement shall be held in strict confidence.

3. TERM
This Agreement commences on 2024-12-19 and shall remain in effect until 2027-06-28, unless terminated earlier.

4. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Delaware.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 60 days written notice.
