MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT

Effective Date: 2024-01-03

The parties to this agreement are:
1. Quantum Research Labs, a limited partnership registered in the Cayman Islands
2. Photon Dynamics, a company incorporated in Netherlands

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Quantum Research Labs shall deliver professional services as described in Schedule A. Photon Dynamics shall remit payment within 45 days of receipt of each invoice.

2. NON-DISCLOSURE
Both parties covenant and agree that all proprietary or confidential information shared during the engagement shall be held in strict confidence.

3. TERM
This Agreement commences on 2024-01-03 and shall remain in effect until 2027-04-13, unless terminated earlier.

4. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Netherlands.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 90 days written notice.
