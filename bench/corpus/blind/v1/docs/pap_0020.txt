Chinchilla: Training Compute-Optimal LLMs

Jordan Hoffmann

Abstract
We present a novel approach to chinchilla. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Main Results:
1. Our model achieves FID 2.93 on the primary benchmark.
2. Performance improves with scale following a power law.
3. The proposed method outperforms all baselines by a significant margin.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 20 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 4.97 and sFID 4.61. The precision score is 0.46.
