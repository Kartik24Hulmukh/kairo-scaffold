EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This agreement is made on the 3rd day of June, 2024.

BETWEEN:
Sigma Technologies Inc., a Delaware corporation (the "Company")
- and -
Jane Marie Doe (the "Employee")

WHEREAS the Company desires to employ the Employee and the Employee desires to accept such employment.

1. DUTIES AND OBLIGATIONS
The Employee shall serve as Chief Technology Officer and shall perform duties as may be assigned by the Board. The Employee agrees to devote substantially all of her professional time to the Company. Salary shall be paid bi-weekly at the rate of $250,000 per annum.

2. CONFIDENTIALITY AND IP
The Employee acknowledges that all inventions, designs, and proprietary information created during employment are the exclusive property of the Company. The Employee shall not disclose any confidential information to third parties, during or after employment, for an indefinite period.

3. TERM OF EMPLOYMENT
Employment shall commence on June 3, 2024 and continue until terminated by either party with ninety (90) days notice.

4. APPLICABLE LAW
New York law governs this agreement, without regard to conflict of laws principles.
