PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

Effective Date: 2024-07-05

The parties to this agreement are:
1. Pinnacle Energy Corp, a limited partnership registered in the Cayman Islands
2. Summit Power Ltd, a company incorporated in Texas

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
Pinnacle Energy Corp shall deliver professional services as described in Schedule A. Summit Power Ltd shall remit payment within 90 days of receipt of each invoice.

2. NON-DISCLOSURE
Both parties covenant and agree that all proprietary or confidential information shared during the engagement shall be held in strict confidence.

3. TERM
This Agreement commences on 2024-07-05 and shall remain in effect until 2029-03-02, unless terminated earlier.

4. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of Texas.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice.
