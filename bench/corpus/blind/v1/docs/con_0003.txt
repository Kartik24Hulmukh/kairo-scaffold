PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT

Effective Date: 2024-02-01

The parties to this agreement are:
1. BlueOcean Ventures LP, a limited partnership registered in the Cayman Islands
2. GreenField Capital Ltd, a company incorporated in Ireland

RECITALS
The parties wish to form a strategic partnership for joint investment in renewable energy projects.

ARTICLE I — CAPITAL CONTRIBUTIONS
Each partner shall contribute capital within 60 days of signing. BlueOcean shall contribute $5,000,000. GreenField shall contribute $3,000,000. Additional capital calls require unanimous consent.

ARTICLE II — CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS
All financial data, investor information, and proprietary strategies disclosed between partners are deemed strictly confidential. Neither party shall divulge such information to any third party without express written consent. This provision survives dissolution of the partnership.

ARTICLE III — DURATION
This partnership begins on February 1, 2024 and ends on January 31, 2034 (ten-year term).

ARTICLE IV — GOVERNING LAW
This agreement is subject to the laws of Ireland.
