Diffusion Models Beat GANs on Image Synthesis

Prafulla Dhariwal, Alex Nichol

Abstract
We present a novel approach to diffusion models beat gans on image synthesis. Our method demonstrates significant improvements over baseline approaches across multiple benchmarks.

Key Claims:
1. Our model achieves 0.93 accuracy on the test set.
2. We show that the proposed method is more efficient than prior work.

Methodology
We use a transformer-based architecture with 14 layers and attention mechanisms.

Experimental Results
On the primary benchmark, our model achieves FID 3.37 and sFID 3.89. The precision score is 0.11.
